My journey

Highschool, 2022, right after COVID-19. JavaScript was my first language, however, I learned more due to how interesting I found coding to be. A-Frame. Python. SQL. Ruby. Lua. Although my application of these was lacking, it sparked my love for coding.

2023 was the year I fully understood pseudocode. I understood a certain pattern recognition throughout all of the programming languages I had learned in the previous year, making my journey a lot easier. Despite this, I understood that I was only at the beginning.

2024. As it is about to end, I have progressed immensely in this wonderful endeavor. I picked my butt up and decided to actually use all of the code I learned. This website, for example, is a practical application of my knowledge. So, as I am sure you've seen, please indulge yourself in my work. Enjoy.
